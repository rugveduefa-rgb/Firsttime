Our Story

A Journey Rooted in Quality

Two years ago, we embarked on a journey to bring exceptional bread to Konkan. Living in Dapoli, we noticed a gap—access to high-quality, artisan bread was limited. We saw an opportunity not just to fill that gap, but to elevate the entire experience of what bread could be in this region.

Our approach is deliberate and uncompromising. We work in small batches, never more than we can give our full attention to. Each loaf is crafted using the finest ingredients we can source—from premium flours to our carefully maintained sourdough cultures.

The sourdough process itself is an art of patience. Through natural fermentation, we develop complex flavors and create bread that's not only more digestible but also richer in nutrients. Time is our ally here—we don't rush the process, allowing the dough to develop at its own pace, resulting in that distinctive tangy flavor, open crumb, and perfectly crisp crust that sourdough is known for.

Our Process

Fermentation

We begin with our natural sourdough starter, a living culture carefully nurtured over time. The dough ferments slowly over 12-24 hours, allowing wild yeasts and beneficial bacteria to work their magic, developing complex flavors and breaking down gluten naturally for better digestibility.

Shaping & Proofing

Each loaf is hand-shaped with care and intention. We then allow it to proof gently in a controlled environment, respecting the dough's natural timeline rather than rushing the process. This patient approach ensures optimal structure and flavor development.

Baking

In our professional convection oven, each loaf bakes to golden perfection. The controlled heat creates that signature crisp, caramelized crust while keeping the interior soft, airy, and full of character. The result is bread that looks as beautiful as it tastes.

What Makes Us Different

Small Batch Craftsmanship

We never mass-produce. Every loaf receives personal attention and care from start to finish. By keeping our batches small, we maintain complete control over quality and ensure that each bread meets our exacting standards. This is artisan baking in its truest form.

Premium Ingredients

We source the finest flours and ingredients available, with a commitment to quality over cost. Our sourdough culture is maintained daily with care. We believe that exceptional bread starts with exceptional ingredients, and we're uncompromising in our choices.

Traditional Sourdough Method

We use only natural starters and long fermentation times. No commercial yeast, no shortcuts, no additives. This traditional approach creates superior flavor, better nutrition, improved digestibility, and bread that stays fresh longer. It's the way bread was meant to be made.

Elevating Konkan

Dapoli is our home, and we're passionate about bringing world-class artisan bread to this beautiful region. We bake with the rhythms of this land, the climate, the seasons. Our mission is to make exceptional sourdough accessible right here in Konkan.
