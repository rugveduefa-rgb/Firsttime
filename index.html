Our Process

[Process Step 1]

Fermentation

We start with a natural sourdough starter, carefully nurtured over time. The dough ferments slowly, allowing flavors to develop and the gluten structure to strengthen naturally.

[Process Step 2]

Shaping & Proofing

Each loaf is hand-shaped with care. We then allow it to proof gently, respecting the dough's natural timeline rather than rushing the process.

[Process Step 3]

Baking

In our wood-fired oven, each loaf bakes to golden perfection. The high heat creates that perfect crust while keeping the interior soft and airy.

What Makes Us Different

Natural Fermentation

We use only natural starters and long fermentation times. No commercial yeast, no shortcuts. This creates better flavor, better digestion, and bread that lasts longer.

Local & Organic

We source the best ingredients we can find, with a commitment to supporting local farmers and using organic flour whenever possible.

Small Batch, High Quality

We don't mass-produce. Every loaf receives attention and care. Quality over quantity is our philosophy, always.

Connection to Place

Napoli in Konkan is our home. We bake with the rhythms of this land, the climate, the seasons. This place flows into every loaf.
